## Welcome!

I lead teams that build machine learning and AI systems. What really excites me is creating tech that makes a genuine difference or tackles real-world problems.

On this blog, I share stories and lessons from my time leading data science and engineering teams at companies like Monolith, Elsevier, and IBM. We've tackled interesting challenges like personalised healthcare, financial machine learning, and even recommendation systems for academic research.

Think of this space as a look into the practical side of AI: navigating the challenges of using machine learning in organisations, seeing how software development is evolving, and exploring how to build and lead data science teams that thrive.
